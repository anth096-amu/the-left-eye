Particularities

Extendable aerial combos

Bayonetta has dozens combos. The longer Bayonetta wants to continue her combo, the harder it is.

She will take advantage on each of your single mistakes to drag you in her combo, dealing a lot of damage.

Excellent edge-guarding

The Umbra witch will take advantage of you being offstage whether by using the Bullet Arts Extension, by catching you with her strongest aerial (Back Air), or by spiking you with her fearsome Heel Stomp.

Special moves mechanics

Bayonetta can use her Side Bs and her Up Bs multiple times depending on the situation.

  • From the ground, or after jumping from the ground, she has 1 Up B and 1 Side B left.
  • If she hits something with her first Side B, she still has 1 Side B left.
  • If she double jumps, she has 1 Up B left.

These rules allow the following schemes :

  • Up B > Jump > Up B
  • Up B > Side B > Jump > Up B
  • Up B > Side B (hits) > Side B > Jump > Up B

In addition of the precedent rules, it is possible to save a Jump by performing a Jump Cancel.

Learning curve

Bayonetta has quite the slow learning curve. If you want to be bayo people, you'll have to learn all of the character's mechanics.

For my personal experience, it took me about more than one month to get satisfying results with this character.

E-Sport

Tier

Bayonetta is often considered as a decent mid-tier character.

Should I pick Bayonetta as a main ?

If all you want is to have fun in SSBU, then yes, go for it. Bayonetta is one the most fun characters to play.

However, Bayonetta lacks consistency. Some combos will work or won't work depending on SDI (smash directional influence).

She also has a ton of landing lag, contrary to most of the cast, and that will be fustrating if you want to be TOP 1 (read more about landing lag in the frame data).

Tips

What is your experience with smash ?

▸ Newbie

If this is your very first hours with the Super Smash Bros. Series, I highly discourage you to learn how to play Bayonetta.

Characters like Mario and Lucina are more beginner-friendly.

▸ Intermediate

  • Learn her most basic combos.
  • Get acknowledge with her frame data.
  • Be aware that Bayonetta gets more landing lag the more she uses her special moves.
